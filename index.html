Our Story

...



Artisan Dessert is a high-end icecream brand. We started our business in 1880. Artisan Dessert believes that making icecream involves both art and science. All of our icecreams are hand-made in a traditional way. All ingredients are organic certified. With Artisan Dissert's brand on, quality is guaranteed.

Our Products

...



We offer a variety of choices, from fruit-based icecream bars to nut-blended icecream scoops, from tea-flavored popsicles to flower-mixed icecream cake. Artisan Dessert experiments with a combination of seasonal fruits, flowers and other fresh ingredience. With our creativity and passion, we are able to deliver the best icecream taste to our customers.

Our Service

...



We serve restaurants and provide catering to all kinds of events and special occasions such as weddings, birthday parties, business banquets and community activities, etc. For the store location and the areas that we serve, please check the contact us page.
